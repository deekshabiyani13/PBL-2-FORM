Explainable AI for IoT Security

This project builds an intelligent intrusion detection system and evaluates the reliability of AI explanations using a novel disagreement analyzer.

Problem Statement

Traditional IoT systems rely on static authentication and lack trust validation. Even highly accurate AI models may produce inconsistent explanations. This research focuses on measuring and improving explanation reliability.

Methodology

Data preprocessing (225K+ samples)

Random Forest model

LIME & SHAP explanations

Disagreement Analyzer

Results

Accuracy 0.9999
Agreement 0.3617
Disagreement 0.6383

Despite very high accuracy, explainability analysis shows high disagreement. This proves that accuracy alone is not enough — trust evaluation is essential.

Key Contribution

A novel disagreement analyzer compares LIME and SHAP explanations using:

Overlap + Rank Correlation + Cosine Similarity

Final Similarity = (Overlap + Rank + Cosine) / 3
Disagreement = 1 − Similarity
